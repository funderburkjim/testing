On hearing this the king and the crow were pacified (lit. were themsolves again). The parrot, too, got up and went away. He was, however, brought bask by Obkravaka, and after matters had been explained to him, was presented with gold ornaments and the like and dismissed, upon which he departed. The parrot having returned to the Vindhya mountain paid his rospects to his sovereign. Seeing him the king Chitraverna asked him-Whas is the news? What sort of country is that?
The parrot replied
Sire, in brief this is the news. Let preparations be made for war. As for the country of Karpu'radvipa, it is (as it were ) a part of heaven; how can it be possible to describe it? The King, on hearing this, sammoned all the principal persons together and sat down to deliberate with them.
